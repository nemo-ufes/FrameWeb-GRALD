Welcome to GRALD Portal!

GRALD is motivated by the growing publication of Linked Data in various domains. GRALD is based on RISCOOS approach , in which goal modeling and risks analysis can be applied.

GRALD also seeks to support the task of choosing vocabularies following the best practices suggested by the W3C Practices for Publishing Linked Data.
